Ruby Ore is a rare ore that generates underground and is a reliable source of ruby.

Obtaining

Breaking

The ruby ore block itself (rather than its ruby drops) can be obtained by mining it with an emerald pickaxe or above with the Silk Toucg enchantment. When mined without Silk Touch, ruby ore drops a single ruby. The maximum amount of rubies dropped can be increased with Fortune.

Natural Generation

Ruby ore can generate in the Overworld in the form of ore features. Ruby ore attempts to generate next to lava 3 times per chunk in features of 1-8 ore, betwwen Y levels 6 to 18 in all biomes. It can also be found in Mining Paradise, between Y levels 6 and 18.
