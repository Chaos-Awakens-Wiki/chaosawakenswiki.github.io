Amethyst Ore is a rare ore that generates deep underground and is a reliable source of amethyst.

Obtaining

Breaking

The amethyst ore block itself (rather than its amethyst drops) can be obtained by mining it with a diamond pickaxe, emerald pickaxe or above with the Silk Touch enchantment. When mined without Silk Touch, amethyst ore drops a single amethyst. The maximum amount of amethysts dropped can be increased with Fortune.

Natural Generation

Amethyst ore can generate in the Overworld in the form of amethyst geodes. Amethyst geodes generate between Y levels 8 to 24.


Amethyst ore can also generate in the Mining Paradise in the form of ore features. Amethyst ore attempts to generate 4 times per chunk in features of 1-4 ore, between Y levels 24 and 56.
