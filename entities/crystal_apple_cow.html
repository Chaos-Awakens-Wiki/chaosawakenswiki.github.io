Crystal Apple Cow

A Crystal Apple Cow is a passive mob found in the Crystal World. It is a source of apples, crystal apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

Crystal apple cows spawn above crystal grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterwards.

Herds of four crystal apple cows spawn on crystal grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above in all Crystal World biomes.

Most crystal apple cows spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a calf (baby cow).

Drops

An adult crystal apple cow drops:

  • 1 to 3 raw beef (or steak if killed while on fire), with a maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 6 with Looting III.
  • 0 to 2 leather, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 2 to 4 apples, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 1 to 2 crystal apple, if killed by the player or tamed wolf.
  • 1-3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf or 1-7 experience orbs upon a successful breeding.

Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.
