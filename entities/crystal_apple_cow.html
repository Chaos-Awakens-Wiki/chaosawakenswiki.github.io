Crystal Apple Cows are a passive mob found in the Crystal World. They are a source of apples, crystal apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

Crystal apple cows spawn above crystal grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterward.

Herds of four crystal apple cows spawn on crystal grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above in all Crystal World biomes.

Most crystal apple cows spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a calf (baby cow).

Drops

Breeding

  • 1-7 experience orbs upon a successful breeding.

On death

An adult crystal apple cow drops:

  • 1-3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf.

Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.

Milking

An adult crystal apple cow can be milked by using a bucket on it, yielding a milk bucket.

Behaviour

A crystal apple cow moos and huffs occasionally while wandering aimlessly, but tends to stay on well lit and grassy places. They avoid water unless following a player and always avoid rails and falls if it warrants damage. If harmed, crystal apple cows flee for a few seconds.


Crystal apple cows follow players holding wheat within a six block radius.

Breeding

A crystal apple cow follows a player who holds wheat but stops following if separated from the player by at least six blocks.


Adult crystal apple cows enter love mode and pair off to create calves after they are given wheat, granting the player one to seven experience orbs. The parent apple cows have a cooldown of five minutes before they can breed again and the growth of calves can be accelerated using wheat; each use reduces the remaining growth duration by 10%.


Baby crystal apple cows take 20 minutes to grow up and follow adult crystal apple cows, preferring them instead of a player holding wheat. The calf that results from breeding attempts to go to the nearest available block to its parent, even if no path can be found. A calf pathfinds to another adult cow if it is separated from its parent by more than 24 blocks.
