Apple Cow

An Apple Cow is a passive mob found in the Overworld and Village Mania. It is a source of apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

Apple cows spawn above grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterwards. Their spawn rate varies per biome.

Herds of four apple cows spawn on grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above, in plains biomes in the Overworld, or in the village plains biome in Village Mania.

Most apple cows spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a calf (baby cow).
