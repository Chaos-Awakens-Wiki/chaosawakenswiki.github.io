Crystal Carrot Pigs are a passive mob found in the Crystal World. They are a source of carrots, crystal carrots and porkchops.

Spawning

Crystal carrot pigs spawn above crystal grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterwards.

Crystal carrot pigs spawn on grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above in all Crystal World biomes.

Most crystal carrot pigs spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a piglet (baby pig).

Drops

Breeding

  • 1-7 experience orbs upon a successful breeding.

On death

An adult crystal carrot pig drops:

Item Roll Chance Quantity (Roll Chance)
Default Looting I Looting II Looting III
Raw Porkchop[1] 100% 1-3 2-4 3-5 4-6
Carrot 100% 2-4 3-5 4-6 5-7
Crystal Carrot 100% 2-4 3-5 4-6 5-7
  1. ↑Dropped as cooked porkchop if on fire when killed.
  • 1-3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf.

Killing a piglet yields no items nor experience.

Behaviour

Crystal carrot pigs roam the world avoiding water unless following a player and always avoid rails and falls if it warrants damage. If harmed, crystal carrot pigs flee for a few seconds.


Crystal carrot pigs follow players holding crystal beetroot or crystal potato within a six block radius.

Breeding

A crystal carrot pig follows a player who holds crystal beetroot or crystal potato but stops following if separated from the player by at least six blocks.


Adult crystal carrot pigs enter love mode and pair off to create piglets after they are given crystal beetroot or crystal potato, granting the player one to seven experience orbs. The parent carrot pigs have a cooldown of five minutes before they can breed again and the growth of piglets can be accelerated using crystal beetroot or crystal potato; each use reduces the remaining growth duration by 10%.


Baby crystal carrot pigs take 20 minutes to grow up and follow adult crystal carrot pigs, preferring them instead of a player holding beetroot or potato. The piglet that results from breeding attempts to go to the nearest available block to its parent, even if no path can be found. A piglet pathfinds to another adult crystal carrot pig if it is separated from its parent by more than 24 blocks.
