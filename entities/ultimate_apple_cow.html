Ultimate Apple Cows are a passive mob. They can only be obtained through breeding. They are a source of apples, golden apples, enchanted golden apples, ultimate apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

The Ultimate apple cow may be obtained by breeding 2 Enchanted Golden Apple Cows together at a 1 in 1000 chance.

Drops

On death

An adult ultimate apple cow drops:

Default Looting I Looting II Looting III

  1. ↑Dropped as steak if on fire when killed.
  • 1-3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf.

Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.

Milking

An adult ultimate apple cow can be milked by using a bucket on it, yielding a milk bucket.

Behaviour

An ultimate apple cow moos and huffs occasionally while wandering aimlessly, but tends to stay on well lit and grassy places. They avoid water unless following a player and always avoid rails and falls if it warrants damage. If harmed, ultimate apple cows flee for a few seconds.

Breeding

Unlike other cows, ultimate apple cows cannot be bred to get more ultimate apple cows.
