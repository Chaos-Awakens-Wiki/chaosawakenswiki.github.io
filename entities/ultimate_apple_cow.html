Ultimate Apple Cows are a passive mob. They can only be obtained through breeding. They are a source of apples, golden apples, enchanted golden apples, ultimate apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

The Ultimate apple cow may be obtained by breeding 2 Enchanted Golden Apple Cows together at a 1 in 1000 chance. The "enableEnchantedAnimalBreeding" config option must be enabled.

Drops

An adult ultimate apple cow drops:

  • 1 to 10 raw beef (or steak if killed while on fire), with a maximum amount is increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 13 with Looting III.
  • 1 to 5 leather, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 8 with Looting III.
  • 3 to 6 apples, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 9 with Looting III.
  • 1 to 3 golden apples, if killed by the player or tamed wolf.
  • 1 to 2 enchanted golden apples, if killed by the player or tamed wolf, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 1 ultimate apple, if killed by the player or tamed wolf, with a maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 4 with Looting III.

Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.
