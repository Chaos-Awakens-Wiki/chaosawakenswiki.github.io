Hercules Beetles are a hostile mob found in mountainous biomes in the Overworld and Mining Paradise. They are a source of diamond and tiger's eye armor, gemstones, and Big Hammers.

Spawning

Hercules Beetles are found in mountainous biomes. Normal Hercules Beetles spawn in Dense Mountain biomes in Mining Paradise, while Throwback Hercules Beetles spawn in mountain biomes in the Overworld.
