Apple Cows are a passive mob found in the Overworld and Village Mania. They are a source of apples, leather, beef, and milk.

Spawning

Apple cows spawn above grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterwards. Their spawn rate varies per biome.

Herds of four apple cows spawn on grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above, in plains biomes in the Overworld, or in the village plains biome in Village Mania.

Most apple cows spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a calf (baby cow).

Drops

An adult apple cow drops:

  • 1 to 3 raw beef (or steak if killed while on fire), with the maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 6 with Looting III.
  • 0 to 2 leather, with the maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 2 to 4 apples, with the maximum amount increased by 1 per level of Looting, for a maximum of 5 with Looting III.
  • 1-3 experience orbs are dropped when killed by a player or tamed wolf or 1-7 experience orbs upon a successful breeding.

Killing a calf yields no items nor experience.
