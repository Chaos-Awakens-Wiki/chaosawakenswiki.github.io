Enchanted Golden Carrot Pigs are a passive mob found in the Overworld and Village Mania. They are a source of golden carrots, carrots and porkchops.

Spawning

Enchanted golden carrot pigs spawn above grass blocks in small herds during world generation or individually afterwards. Their spawn rate varies per biome.

Herds of three enchanted golden carrot pigs spawn on grass blocks at a light level of 9 or higher with at least two blocks of space above, in plains biomes in the Overworld, or in the village plains biome in Village Mania.

Most enchanted golden carrot pigs spawn as adults, but they have a 5% chance of spawning as a piglet (baby pig).

Drops

Breeding

  • 1-7 experience orbs upon a successful breeding.

On death

An adult enchanted golden carrot pig drops:

Behaviour

Enchanted golden carrot pigs roam the world avoiding water unless following a player and always avoid rails and falls if it warrants damage. If harmed, enchanted golden carrot pigs flee for a few seconds.


Enchanted golden carrot pigs follow players holding beetroot or potato within a six block radius.

Breeding

A enchanted golden carrot pig follows a player who holds beetroot or potato but stops following if separated from the player by at least six blocks.


Adult enchanted golden carrot pigs enter love mode and pair off to create piglets after they are given beetroot or potato, granting the player one to seven experience orbs. The parent enchanted golden carrot pigs have a cooldown of five minutes before they can breed again and the growth of piglets can be accelerated using beetroot or potato; each use reduces the remaining growth duration by 10%.


If the "enableEnchantedAnimalBreeding" config value is set to false (default value is false). Piglets obtained by breeding enchanted golden carrot pigs will nearly always be golden carrot pigs.
If the config value has been set to true, piglets obtained by breeding enchanted golden carrot pigs will nearly always be enchanted golden carrot pigs.


Baby enchanted golden carrot pigs take 20 minutes to grow up and follow adult enchanted golden carrot pigs, preferring them instead of a player holding beetroot or potato. The piglet that results from breeding attempts to go to the nearest available block to its parent, even if no path can be found. A piglet pathfinds to another adult enchanted golden carrot pig if it is separated from its parent by more than 24 blocks.
