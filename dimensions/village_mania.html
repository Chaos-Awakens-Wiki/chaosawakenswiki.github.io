The Village Mania is a dimension containing villages, mobs and exclusive structures.

Accessing

Main article: Rainbow Ant

In order to enter the Village Mania dimenion, the player must find a rainbow ant and right-click on it.


Rainbow ants spawn from rainbow ant nests, so try grabbing some rainbow ant nests and putting them near your base for ease of access.


If cheats are enabled, players can also teleport to the Village Mania without right-clicking a rainbow ant: the syntax is /execute in village_mania

Environment

The Village Mania features overworld like biomes containing many villages.
