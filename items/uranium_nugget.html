Uranium Nugget is a rare metal nugget that is mainly used to craft high-tier tools and armor.

Obtaining

Mob loot

Robo Jefferys have a chance of dropping 2-4 uranium nuggets upon death. Robo Pounders have a chance of dropping 1-2 uranium nuggets upon death. Robo Warriors have a chance of dropping 0-1 uranium nuggets upon death.

Trading

If the "wanderingTraderSellsUraniumAndTitanium" config value is set to true (default value is false). Wandering traders may sell a uranium nugget for 15 emeralds each. This trade becomes locked after 3 are bought.

Chest loot

Uranium Nuggets can generate in chests in Ent Dungeons.
